Catalyst: chem_C1
  Name: 对比催化剂 C-1
  Aliases: Catalyst C-1
  Composition:
    Pd: 0.8 wt%
    Pt: 0.4 wt%
  Properties:
    pore_volume: 0.25 ml/g
    surface_area: 152 m2/g

Synthesis Pathway:

  Step 1: EUO Ion Exchange and Calcination (ID: synth_step_2)
    Procedure: 上述合成的沸石含有碱金属或碱土金属的阳离子,与铵阳离子进行交换,随后经过316℃~540°℃的空气中焙烧 1~10小时,此种形成酸性沸石是本领域中熟知的。
    Inputs:
      - Ammonium ion solution (Reagent)
      - EUO分子筛 (Reactant)
        Properties:
          pore_volume: 0.19 mL/g
          silicon_aluminum_molar_ratio: 63 molar ratio
          surface_area: 449 m2/g
    Outputs:
      - 氢型EUO分子筛
    Conditions:
      atmosphere: Air atmosphere
      temperature: 316~540 C
      time: 1~10 h

  Step 2: Support S-1 Mixing and Extrusion (ID: synth_step_3)
    Procedure: 将步骤(1)所得的酸性EUO分子筛、氧化铝粉充分混合,加入66wt%的硝酸混捏成可塑的膏状物,挤成直径为 1.5mm 的圆柱条
    Inputs:
      - Water (Solvent/Mixing medium)
      - 氢型EUO分子筛 (Component)
      - 氧化铝粉 (Component)
      - 硝酸 (Peptizing agent)
        Properties:
          concentration: 66 wt%
    Outputs:
      - Extruded EUO/Alumina paste
        Properties:
          diameter: 1.5 mm

  Step 3: Support S-1 Drying (ID: synth_step_4)
    Procedure: 经过110°℃干燥8h
    Inputs:
      - Extruded EUO/Alumina paste (Material to be dried)
        Properties:
          diameter: 1.5 mm
    Outputs:
      - Dried support intermediate
    Conditions:
      temperature: 110 C
      time: 8 h

  Step 4: Support S-1 Calcination (ID: synth_step_5)
    Procedure: 然后在空气气氛中550℃焙烧6h
    Inputs:
      - Dried support intermediate (Material to be calcined)
    Outputs:
      - 载体 S-1
        Composition:
          Alumina: 40 wt%
          EUO molecular sieve: 60 wt%
        Properties:
          diameter: 1.35 mm
    Conditions:
      atmosphere: Air atmosphere
      temperature: 550 C
      time: 6 h

  Step 5: C-1 Impregnation (ID: synth_step_6)
    Procedure: 使用含有 Pt(NH2)2C12 和 Pd(NH2)4(NO3)2 的溶液进行饱和浸渍
    Inputs:
      - Pd(NH2)4(NO3)2 solution (Precursor solution)
      - Pt(NH2)2C12 solution (Precursor solution)
      - 载体 S-1 (Support)
        Composition:
          Alumina: 40 wt%
          EUO molecular sieve: 60 wt%
        Properties:
          diameter: 1.35 mm
    Outputs:
      - Impregnated support for C-1

  Step 6: C-1 Drying (ID: synth_step_7)
    Procedure: 然后在110℃下干燥6h
    Inputs:
      - Impregnated support for C-1 (Material to be dried)
    Outputs:
      - Dried intermediate for C-1
    Conditions:
      temperature: 110 C
      time: 6 h

  Step 7: C-1 Calcination (ID: synth_step_8)
    Procedure: 在空气气氛中380℃焙烧6h
    Inputs:
      - Dried intermediate for C-1 (Material to be calcined)
    Outputs:
      - 对比催化剂 C-1
        Composition:
          Pd: 0.8 wt%
          Pt: 0.4 wt%
        Properties:
          pore_volume: 0.25 ml/g
          surface_area: 152 m2/g
    Conditions:
      atmosphere: Air atmosphere
      temperature: 380 C
      time: 6 h
